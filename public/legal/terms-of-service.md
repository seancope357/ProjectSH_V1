## 5. Commission Structure and Fees

### 5.1 Platform Commission

SequenceHub charges a **10% platform fee** on all sequence sales. This fee covers:
- Payment processing
- Digital file hosting and delivery
- Platform maintenance and development
- Customer support
- Security and fraud prevention

### 5.2 Fee Calculation

For each sale:
- **Buyer** pays the full listed price
- **Platform** retains 10% as commission
- **Seller** receives 90% of the sale price (minus payment processing fees)

**Example:** If a sequence is sold for $20.00:
- Buyer pays: $20.00
- Platform fee: $2.00 (10%)
- Seller receives: $18.00 (minus Stripe fees, approximately $0.88)
- Seller net: Approximately $17.12

### 5.3 Payment Processing Fees

In addition to the Platform fee, standard payment processing fees apply (typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction). These fees are deducted by our payment processor (Stripe) before funds reach your account.
