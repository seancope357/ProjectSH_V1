## 3. Commission Structure and Fees

### 3.1 Platform Commission

SequenceHub charges a **10% platform commission** on all sequence sales.

**Commission Covers:**
- Payment processing infrastructure
- Digital file hosting and delivery
- Platform maintenance and development
- Seller dashboard and analytics
- Customer support services
- Security and fraud prevention
- Marketing and promotion

### 3.2 Fee Calculation

**Transaction Breakdown:**

For a sequence sold at **$20.00**:
1. **Buyer pays:** $20.00
2. **Platform commission (10%):** $2.00
3. **Stripe processing fee (~2.9% + $0.30):** $0.88
4. **Seller receives:** $17.12 (85.6% of sale price)

**Formula:**
```
Seller Earnings = (Sale Price × 0.90) - Stripe Fees
Stripe Fees = (Sale Price × 0.029) + $0.30
```

### 3.3 Stripe Processing Fees

Standard Stripe fees apply:
- **U.S. cards:** 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **International cards:** 3.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Currency conversion:** Additional 1% fee

**Note:** Stripe fees may vary by region and card type. See Stripe's pricing for your location.

### 3.4 No Hidden Fees

**We do NOT charge:**
- Monthly subscription fees
- Listing fees
- Account maintenance fees
- Withdrawal fees
- Inactivity fees

**You only pay when you make a sale.**

### 3.5 Fee Modifications

SequenceHub reserves the right to modify commission rates:
- **Notice Period:** 30 days advance notice for fee increases
- **Grandfathering:** Existing listings may be grandfathered at old rates (at our discretion)
- **Communication:** Notices sent via email and Platform announcements

**Your Options:**
- Continue selling under new terms
- Remove listings and close seller account
- Negotiate enterprise pricing (high-volume sellers)

## 4. Payout Terms and Schedule

### 4.1 Payout Processing

**Payout Schedule:**
- Processed by Stripe according to your payout schedule
- Default: Rolling basis (typically 2-7 business days after sale)
- Adjustable in Stripe dashboard (daily, weekly, monthly)

**Payout Method:**
- Direct deposit to linked bank account
- Debit card transfer (instant, may incur fees)
- As supported by Stripe in your region

### 4.2 Minimum Payout Threshold

**Threshold:** $1.00 minimum payout
- Earnings below threshold accumulate until reached
- No maximum accumulation period
- First payout may be delayed pending verification

### 4.3 Payout Holds

Payouts may be held or delayed for:
- Initial verification period (up to 14 days for first sale)
- Dispute or chargeback investigation
- Suspected fraudulent activity
- Policy violation investigation
- High-risk transaction patterns
- As required by law or payment processor

**Notification:** You will be notified of holds and the reason.

### 4.4 Reserve Requirements

In certain circumstances, we may require a reserve:
- New sellers with high transaction volumes
- Accounts with elevated chargeback rates
- High-risk categories or patterns
- As required by payment processors

**Reserve Terms:**
- Typically 10-20% of sales held for 30-90 days
- Released on rolling basis
- Adjusted based on account performance
- Reviewed monthly

### 4.5 Chargebacks and Disputes

**Seller Responsibility:**
- Respond promptly to chargeback notices
- Provide evidence to contest invalid chargebacks
- Chargebacks deducted from future earnings

**Excessive Chargebacks:**
- Chargeback rate above 1% may trigger review
- Multiple chargebacks may result in account suspension
- You're responsible for chargeback fees ($15-25 per chargeback)

### 4.6 Unclaimed Funds

Funds in your account:
- Remain available indefinitely while account is active
- Subject to escheatment laws if account closed with balance
- Contact support to retrieve funds after account closure
