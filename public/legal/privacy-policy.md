## 1. Introduction

Welcome to SequenceHub. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our marketplace platform.

**Key Points:**
- We collect information you provide and information about your use of the Platform
- We use your information to provide services, process payments, and improve the Platform
- We share information with service providers and as required by law
- You have rights to access, correct, and delete your information
- We implement security measures to protect your data

By using SequenceHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Platform.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 6.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services.

### 6.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies:**
- Authentication and session management
- Security features
- Platform functionality
- Cannot be disabled without affecting service

**Analytics Cookies:**
- Usage statistics
- Performance monitoring
- Feature effectiveness measurement
- Can be disabled in cookie settings

**Preference Cookies:**
- Remember your settings
- Personalization preferences
- Language and region settings

**Marketing Cookies (Optional):**
- Targeted advertising
- Campaign effectiveness
- Third-party advertising platforms
- Can be disabled in cookie settings

### 6.3 Other Tracking Technologies

**Local Storage:** Used to store user preferences and cached data locally.

**Web Beacons:** Small graphics that help us understand user behavior and email effectiveness.

**Session Storage:** Temporary storage cleared when you close your browser.

### 6.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:
- Browser settings (most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies)
- Our cookie consent tool
- Third-party opt-out mechanisms

**Note:** Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using certain features of the Platform.

### 6.5 Do Not Track

We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals. This policy may change as industry standards develop.

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security audits
- Firewall protection
- DDoS mitigation
- Access controls and authentication

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Employee training on data protection
- Strict access policies (least privilege principle)
- Background checks for personnel with data access
- Incident response procedures

**Third-Party Security:**
- We select service providers with strong security practices
- Regular security assessments of partners
- Contractual data protection obligations

### 7.2 Payment Security

- We do not store full credit card numbers
- Payment data is handled by PCI DSS compliant Stripe
- Tokenization protects payment information

### 7.3 Your Responsibility

To protect your account:
- Choose a strong, unique password
- Do not share your login credentials
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Log out after using shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### 7.4 Security Limitations

**No system is 100% secure.** While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You use the Platform at your own risk.

**Data Breaches:** In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by law.

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 How Long We Keep Data

**Account Information:**
- Retained while your account is active
- Deleted within 90 days of account closure (unless legal obligation requires retention)

**Transaction Data:**
- Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
- Required for legal compliance (financial records)

**Communications:**
- Support tickets retained for 2 years
- Marketing communications retained until you unsubscribe

**Usage Logs:**
- Retained for 12 months
- Aggregated analytics retained indefinitely

**Content (Sequences):**
- Active listings retained while published
- Deleted sequences removed within 30 days (except copies in buyer accounts)

### 8.2 Legal Holds

We may retain information longer if:
- Required by law
- Necessary for legal proceedings
- Needed to enforce our Terms
- Part of an ongoing investigation

### 8.3 Backup Retention

Backup systems may retain deleted data for up to 90 days. Data in backups is not accessible and will be permanently deleted according to backup retention schedules.

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

### 9.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format (JSON, CSV)
- Review what information we hold about you

**How to Exercise:** Contact privacy@sequencehub.com or use your account settings.

### 9.2 Correction and Update

You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate information
- Update outdated information
- Complete incomplete information

**How to Exercise:** Update directly in account settings or contact support.

### 9.3 Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to exceptions:

**We will delete your information unless:**
- Required for legal compliance (tax records, transaction history)
- Necessary to complete transactions
- Needed for security and fraud prevention
- Required to defend legal claims
- Part of backup systems (automatically deleted within 90 days)

**How to Exercise:** Request account deletion through account settings or contact privacy@sequencehub.com.

**Effects of Deletion:**
- Account closure and loss of access
- Removal of public profile
- Forfeiture of pending payouts
- Buyers retain access to previously purchased sequences

### 9.4 Restrict Processing

You may request that we limit how we use your information in certain circumstances.

### 9.5 Object to Processing

You may object to our processing of your information for certain purposes, including marketing.

### 9.6 Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

### 9.7 Marketing Opt-Out

You may unsubscribe from marketing communications:
- Click "unsubscribe" links in emails
- Update preferences in account settings
- Contact privacy@sequencehub.com

**Note:** You will still receive transactional emails (receipts, account notifications) necessary for Platform operation.

### 9.8 Response Time

We respond to privacy requests within 30 days. Complex requests may take up to 60 days, and we will notify you of any delay.

## 13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

### 13.1 Applicability

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

### 13.2 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on:

**Contract Performance:** Processing necessary to provide services you requested.

**Legitimate Interests:** Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests (fraud prevention, analytics, marketing to existing customers).

**Consent:** Processing based on your explicit consent (optional marketing emails, cookies).

**Legal Obligation:** Processing required by law (tax reporting, legal compliance).

### 13.3 GDPR Rights

**Right of Access:** Obtain confirmation and copy of your personal data.

**Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

**Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** Request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.

**Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we process your data in certain situations.

**Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

**Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for consent-based processing at any time.

**Right to Lodge a Complaint:** File a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### 13.4 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise GDPR rights:
- Email: privacy@sequencehub.com or dpo@sequencehub.com
- Use in-platform tools in account settings
- Contact your local data protection authority

**Response Time:** We respond within 30 days (may extend to 60 days for complex requests).

### 13.5 Data Protection Officer

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer:
Email: dpo@sequencehub.com

### 13.6 Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country. Find your authority at: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en
