## 2. Quality Standards for Sequences

### 2.1 Minimum Requirements

All paid sequences must meet these minimum standards:

**Duration:**
- Minimum 30 seconds for paid sequences
- Minimum 15 seconds for preview clips
- Full-length songs recommended (2-4 minutes)

**Functionality:**
- Sequences must work in xLights without errors
- All effects must render properly
- Timing must be synchronized with audio
- No corrupted or incomplete files

**Metadata:**
- Accurate song title and artist
- Correct duration
- List of props/models used
- Specify xLights version compatibility
- Include channel count or prop requirements

**Testing:**
- Test sequences before uploading
- Verify file integrity
- Ensure preview videos match actual sequence
- Check for timing issues or errors

### 2.2 Quality Expectations

While subjective, high-quality sequences should demonstrate:

**Technical Proficiency:**
- Smooth transitions and effects
- Appropriate use of color and intensity
- Well-timed movements synchronized to music
- Professional-looking execution

**Creativity:**
- Original choreography
- Thoughtful effect choices
- Cohesive visual storytelling
- Attention to musical elements (beats, lyrics, tempo)

**Production Value:**
- Clean, polished appearance
- Appropriate preview videos
- Clear, professional descriptions
- High-resolution thumbnails

### 2.3 Pricing Guidelines

Price sequences fairly based on:
- Complexity and effort required
- Length and production value
- Market rates for similar sequences
- Target audience and niche

**Recommended Pricing Ranges:**
- Simple sequences (basic effects, short duration): $5-$15
- Standard sequences (moderate complexity): $15-$30
- Advanced sequences (complex effects, custom props): $30-$75
- Premium/professional sequences: $75+

**Avoid:**
- Artificially inflated prices
- Pricing significantly above market rates without justification
- Misleading "sale" pricing tactics

### 2.4 Preview Materials

**Required:**
- At least one clear thumbnail image
- Accurate representation of sequence content

**Recommended:**
- Preview video showing actual sequence playback (20-60 seconds)
- Multiple preview images from different parts of sequence
- Screen recordings from xLights (optional but helpful)

**Guidelines:**
- Previews must accurately represent the final product
- No misleading or enhanced previews that don't match actual sequence
- Video quality should be at least 720p
- Include watermark if desired to prevent theft

## 3. Content Restrictions

### 3.1 Family-Friendly Requirement

SequenceHub is a family-friendly platform. All sequences must be appropriate for all ages.

**Prohibited Content:**
- Explicit language, profanity, or offensive lyrics
- Sexual or suggestive content
- Violence, gore, or disturbing imagery
- Drug or alcohol references
- Hate symbols or extremist content

**Music Content:**
- Clean/radio edit versions required for songs with explicit lyrics
- Instrumental or classical music always acceptable
- Religious content permitted if respectful and appropriate

### 3.2 Cultural Sensitivity

Respect diverse cultural backgrounds:
- Avoid culturally insensitive or appropriative content
- Be mindful of religious symbols and themes
- Consider international audience perspectives
- Avoid stereotypes or caricatures

### 3.3 Holiday Themes

While Christmas is our primary focus, we welcome:
- Christmas and winter holiday sequences
- Religious Christmas themes (nativity, hymns)
- Secular winter themes (snowflakes, general holiday)
- Other holidays with appropriate categorization (Halloween, Easter, etc.)

**Respectful Representation:**
- Represent religious themes with respect
- Avoid commercialization that may offend
- Be mindful of diverse holiday traditions

### 3.4 Brand and Trademark Usage

**Allowed:**
- Song titles and artist names (factual information)
- General references to products used (e.g., "compatible with xLights")

**Prohibited:**
- Unauthorized use of company logos or trademarks
- Misrepresentation of affiliation with brands
- Use of copyrighted characters without permission (Disney, etc.)
- Trademark infringement

## 4. Copyright and Licensing

### 4.1 Original Work Requirement

You may only upload sequences that you:
- Created yourself from scratch
- Have explicit permission to sell
- Have purchased appropriate licenses for

**Never Upload:**
- Sequences created by others without permission
- Sequences containing unauthorized copyrighted elements
- Work that infringes on others' intellectual property

### 4.2 Music Licensing

**Your Responsibility:**
Sellers are solely responsible for ensuring proper music licensing. Common options:

**Licensed Options:**
- Royalty-free music from licensed providers
- Music you hold sync licensing rights for
- Public domain music (pre-1929 compositions)
- Creative Commons music (check specific license terms)
- Music where you've obtained mechanical and sync rights

**What This Means:**
- Using popular copyrighted music requires licensing
- Radio play rights â‰  sync rights for sequences
- "Fair use" does not typically apply to commercial sequence sales
- You can still use copyrighted music if you have proper licenses

**Resources:**
- Epidemic Sound
- Artlist
- AudioJungle
- Musicbed
- Public Domain 4U

**Platform Protection:**
SequenceHub reserves the right to remove sequences if we receive DMCA takedown notices or become aware of licensing violations.

### 4.3 Attribution Requirements

If using Creative Commons or other licensed content requiring attribution:
- Clearly state attribution in sequence description
- Follow specific license terms
- Link to original creator if required

### 4.4 Respecting Others' Work

**Derivative Works:**
- Creating variations of others' sequences requires permission
- Adding your own effects to someone else's base sequence requires licensing
- Significantly transformative work may be acceptable, but always credit inspiration

**Sequence Borrowing:**
- Don't copy timing or choreography from others' sequences
- Inspiration is fine; replication is not
- When in doubt, create something original

## 5. File Format and Technical Requirements

### 5.1 Supported File Formats

**Required Formats:**
- `.fseq` (xLights Sequence Format) - Required
- `.xsq` (xLights Project File) - Optional but recommended

**Accompanying Files (Optional):**
- Audio files (`.mp3`, `.wav`, `.m4a`)
- Preview videos (`.mp4`, `.mov`)
- Documentation (`.pdf`, `.txt`, `.md`)
- Model files for custom props

### 5.2 File Size Limits

**Maximum File Sizes:**
- Sequence files: 100 MB per file
- Audio files: 25 MB per file
- Video previews: 200 MB per file
- Total upload per sequence: 500 MB

**Optimization:**
- Compress files when possible without quality loss
- Remove unnecessary data
- Use appropriate audio bitrates (128-320 kbps)

### 5.3 File Integrity

Ensure all uploaded files:
- Open correctly in xLights
- Are not corrupted or incomplete
- Contain all necessary data
- Match descriptions and previews

### 5.4 Version Compatibility

**Specify xLights Version:**
- Indicate minimum xLights version required
- Test compatibility with current xLights version
- Note any version-specific features used

**Updates:**
- Update sequences for major xLights releases when possible
- Notify buyers of compatibility issues
- Provide updated versions when feasible

### 5.5 Documentation

**Recommended Inclusions:**
- README file with setup instructions
- Prop/model requirements and specifications
- Installation guide
- Troubleshooting tips
- Credits and attribution

## 6. Seller Conduct and Professionalism

### 6.1 Customer Service Standards

**Response Times:**
- Respond to buyer messages within 48 hours
- Acknowledge support requests promptly
- Provide timely updates on issues

**Communication Quality:**
- Professional and courteous tone
- Clear and helpful explanations
- Patient with less experienced users
- Proactive in resolving issues

### 6.2 Product Support

**Expected Support:**
- Help with basic installation and setup questions

- Assist with file download problems

- Teaching general xLights usage (direct to resources)

Provide complete, accurate information:
- Clear titles that describe content
- Detailed descriptions of effects and features
- Accurate prop/model requirements
- Honest difficulty level assessment

**Avoid:**

- Omitting important requirements
- Unclear or vague descriptions
- Clickbait titles

### 6.4 Portfolio Management

**Keep Your Profile Updated:**
- Maintain accurate seller bio
- Update social media links
- Showcase your best work
- Respond to reviews

**Sequence Management:**
- Remove outdated or unsupported sequences
- Update sequences when improvements are made

- Archive sequences no longer supported

### 6.5 Pricing Integrity

**Pricing Practices:**
- Set fair, consistent prices
- Honor advertised prices
- Avoid frequent drastic price changes
- Clearly mark sales or promotions

**Prohibited:**

## 9. Reviews and Ratings

### 9.1 Purpose of Reviews

Reviews help buyers make informed decisions and help sellers improve their work. Honest, constructive reviews benefit the entire community.

### 9.2 Review Eligibility

**Who Can Review:**
- Buyers who have purchased the sequence
- Verified purchases only
- One review per purchase

**When to Review:**
- After downloading and viewing the sequence
- Ideally after testing in xLights
- When you have sufficient experience with the product

### 9.3 Writing Quality Reviews

**Good Reviews Include:**
- Specific observations about quality
- Assessment of value for price
- Technical details (if applicable)
- Constructive criticism
- Recommendation or context for potential buyers

**Example of Good Review:**
*"Beautiful Christmas sequence with smooth transitions and excellent timing. Props matched my setup perfectly. The seller included clear documentation which was very helpful. Would have liked more variation in the second half, but overall excellent value for $25."*

**Avoid:**
- Vague comments ("good" or "bad" without explanation)
- Personal attacks on seller
- Comments about shipping/download speed (platform issue, not seller)
- Irrelevant information
- Offensive language

### 9.4 Rating Criteria

**5 Stars:** Exceptional quality, exceeds expectations
**4 Stars:** High quality, meets expectations
**3 Stars:** Acceptable quality, meets basic standards
**2 Stars:** Below expectations, significant issues
**1 Star:** Poor quality or major problems

**Be Fair:**
- Consider price point when rating
- Account for complexity level
- Distinguish between personal preference and quality
- Consider seller responsiveness if issues arose

### 9.5 Seller Responses to Reviews

Sellers may respond to reviews:
- Thank buyers for positive feedback
- Address concerns raised in critical reviews
- Offer solutions to reported problems
- Provide clarification

**Seller Response Guidelines:**
- Remain professional and courteous
- Don't argue with or attack reviewers
- Take criticism constructively
- Offer to resolve issues privately

## 11. Consequences for Violations

### 11.1 Warning System

**First Offense (Minor Violations):**
- Warning notification
- Educational resources provided
- Required acknowledgment of guidelines
- Monitoring of future activity

**Second Offense or Moderate Violations:**
- Temporary restriction of privileges
- Removal of violating content
- Suspension from selling for 7-30 days
- Required review of policies

**Third Offense or Serious Violations:**
- Extended suspension (30-90 days)
- Loss of seller privileges
- Removal of all listings
- Potential account termination

### 11.2 Immediate Suspension or Termination

Certain violations result in immediate account action without warning:
- Fraud or payment manipulation
- Severe copyright infringement
- Harassment or threats
- Illegal activity
- Multiple simultaneous violations
- Circumventing previous suspensions

### 11.3 Content Removal

Violating content may be removed immediately:
- The seller is notified of removal
- Specific violation is explained
- Guidance for compliance provided
- Opportunity to correct and re-upload (if applicable)

### 11.4 Payment Withholding

In cases of:
- Fraudulent activity
- Significant policy violations
- Pending investigations
- Chargeback abuse

...we may withhold seller payments until resolution.

### 11.5 Permanent Ban

Accounts may be permanently terminated for:
- Repeated serious violations
- Illegal activity
- Severe abuse of Platform or users
- Creating new accounts to evade suspensions
- Irreparable breach of community trust

**Effects of Permanent Ban:**
- Loss of all Platform access
- Forfeiture of unreleased payments (in fraud cases)
- Removal of all content
- Ban may extend to associated accounts

## 12. Reporting Mechanism

### 12.1 How to Report Violations

**Report Buttons:**
- Available on all sequence listings
- Available on reviews and comments
- Available in message threads

**Email Support:**
- support@sequencehub.com for general issues
- abuse@sequencehub.com for serious violations
- legal@sequencehub.com for intellectual property claims

### 12.2 What to Include in Reports

**Effective Reports Contain:**
- Clear description of the violation
- Specific guideline or policy violated
- Link to offending content
- Screenshots or evidence (if applicable)
- Your contact information for follow-up
- Date and time of incident

### 12.3 Report Processing

**What Happens:**
1. Report is reviewed by moderation team
2. Evidence is collected and assessed
3. Involved parties may be contacted
4. Determination is made
5. Appropriate action is taken
6. Reporter is notified of outcome (when appropriate)

**Timeline:**
- Minor issues: 2-3 business days
- Urgent issues: 24 hours
- Intellectual property claims: 5-7 business days

### 12.4 False Reports

Abuse of reporting system (filing false or malicious reports) may result in:
- Warning
- Reporting privilege restrictions
- Account suspension

### 12.5 DMCA Copyright Claims

**For Copyright Infringement:**
- Use designated DMCA process
- Email: legal@sequencehub.com
- Include all required DMCA information
- Provide proof of ownership or authorization

**DMCA Requirements:**
- Identify copyrighted work
- Identify infringing material
- Provide contact information
- Include good faith statement
- Include accuracy statement under penalty of perjury
- Physical or electronic signature

## 13. Appeal Process

### 13.1 Right to Appeal

If your content is removed or your account is suspended, you have the right to appeal the decision.

### 13.2 How to Appeal

**Submit Appeal Via:**
- Email: appeals@sequencehub.com
- Include "APPEAL" in subject line
- Reference your account name and the action being appealed

**Appeal Should Include:**
- Clear explanation of why you believe the action was incorrect
- Any relevant evidence or context
- Acknowledgment of guidelines and commitment to compliance
- Proposed resolution

### 13.3 Appeal Review Process

**Steps:**
1. Appeal is reviewed by different team member than original decision
2. All evidence and context is reconsidered
3. Additional information may be requested
4. Final determination is made
5. You are notified of the outcome

**Timeline:**
- Standard appeals: 5-10 business days
- Urgent appeals: 2-3 business days
- Complex appeals: Up to 30 days

### 13.4 Appeal Outcomes

**Possible Results:**
- Action overturned (content restored, account reinstated)
- Action modified (reduced suspension, partial restoration)
- Action upheld (original decision stands)
- Additional action taken (if new violations discovered)

### 13.5 Final Decisions

**Second Appeal:**
- Available for serious actions (account termination)
- Reviewed by senior management
- Decision is final

**No Further Appeals:**
- After second appeal decision
- For clearly documented violations
- When action is legally required
